The Christian Union Activivtes

Fellowship

Brother's fellowship

Men in the christian union meet to discuss their issues in this fellowship. They discuss issues facing them as they journey through the faith. They also plan for their activites to be done in the semester in this fellowship.

Sister's fellowship

They are the christian ladies in the christian union. They focus on their journey as believers and share with each other their experiences since they recieved salvation. They help each other to grow.

Missions

Outside campus evangelism

The christian union has an evangelism team that goes an extra mile outside the campus to preach the gospel to the nation. They reach the greator community to save the dying souls outside.

Door to door evangelism

The evangelist set on a journey to reach out to fellow students. They preach from door to door and from hostel to hostel. They reach out to students in the campus with the gospel of power.
